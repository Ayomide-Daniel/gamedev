How to play

Click to choose any of the objects, Rock ✊ | Paper ✋ | Scissors ✌️.
To win the game, you should know these:

  • Rock crushs Scissors
  • Paper covers Rock
  • Scissors cuts Paper
  • Choosing the same object with the computer results in a Draw!
  • Click Next Round! to move to the next round.
  • Lastly, to win the game you must win 7 rounds.
